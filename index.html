SIGSTOP Logic

Unlike task killers, FrostByte tells the kernel to pause the app. No data lost, no windows closed. It just stays on ice.

Lazy Thaw

Focusing a tab-heavy browser? FrostByte wakes the active process instantly and thaws others sequentially to avoid CPU spikes.

Audio Smart

Playing music? On a Zoom call? FrostByte automatically detects active streams and keeps them running. Period.
