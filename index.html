RAM on Ice.

A zero-config background daemon that auto-freezes idle applications to reclaim gigabytes of memory. Transparent, fast, and built for GNOME & Wayland.

See it in action

Watch how FrostByte identifies idle processes and puts them into a "Frozen" state. The memory is instantly marked as free by the Linux kernel.

Auto-Suspend

Monitors CPU patterns. When an app stays idle for 10 minutes, it gets a SIGSTOP signal. RAM is reclaimed, but the app stays ready.

Lazy Thaw

Focusing a heavy app? Only the main window thaws instantly. Child tabs (Chrome, VS Code) wake up one-by-one to keep the UI snappy.

Audio Aware

Never breaks your flow. FrostByte detects active audio streams via PipeWire/PulseAudio and automatically whitelists those apps.

Engineered for GNOME

01.

Transparent integration via a companion GNOME Shell Extension. It tracks window focus and click events for near-zero latency wake-ups.

02.

Handles complex process trees. Whether it's a deep hierarchy of terminal processes or an Electron beast, FrostByte keeps it tidy.

03.

Pure Python. No heavy runtimes, no complex dependencies. Just a fast, efficient background service managed by systemd.
